1. https://www.php.net/downloads.php
2. Create folder PHP8 in C:
3. Copy zip to PHP8
4. Environment Variables: PATH, C:\PHP8
5. Copy the C:\PHP8\php.ini-development to C:\PHP8\php.ini
6. php.ini:
   memory_limit from 128M to 1G
   Uncomment extension_dir = "ext"
   extension=mysqli
   extension=pdo_mysql
   extension=pdo_pgsql
   extension=pgsql
